Guidelines for Nonferrous Scrap: Commodity Specifications

Shall consist of No. 1 bare, uncoated, unalloyed copper wire, commonly known as Bare Bright copper wire. Wire gauge subject to agreement between buyer and seller. Green copper wire and hydraulically compacted material to be subject to agreement.

Shall consist of clean, untinned, uncoated, unalloyed copper wire and cable, free of brittle burnt wire. Wire gauge subject to agreement between buyer and seller. Free of copper tubing. Hydraulically briquetted copper subject to agreement.

Shall consist of miscellaneous, unalloyed copper wire having a nominal 96% copper content (minimum 94%) as determined by electrolytic assay. Should be free of excessively leaded, tinned, or soldered copper wire; brass and bronze wire; excessive oil, iron, and non-metallics; copper wire from burning; insulation; hair wire; brittle burnt wire; and should be reasonably free of ash. Hydraulically briquetted copper subject to agreement.

Shall consist of clean, unalloyed, uncoated copper clippings, punchings, bus bars, commutator segments, and clean copper tubing. Hydraulically briquetted copper subject to agreement.

A combination of copper wire and heavy copper as defined in Berry and Candy. See above.

Shall consist of miscellaneous, unalloyed copper scrap having a nominal 96% copper content (minimum 94%) as determined by electrolytic assay. Should be free of excessively leaded, tinned, or soldered copper scrap; brasses and bronzes; excessive oil, iron, and non-metallics; copper tubing with non-copper connections or sediment; copper wire from burning; insulation; hair wire; brittle burnt wire; and should be reasonably free of ash. Hydraulically briquetted copper subject to agreement.

A combination of No. 2 copper wire and copper as defined in Birch and Cliff. See above.

Shall consist of No. 1 bare, uncoated, unalloyed copper wire scrap nodules, chopped or shredded, free of tin, lead, zinc, aluminum, iron, other metallic impurities, insulation, and foreign contamination. Minimum copper 99%. Gauge smaller than No. 16 B & S wire and hydraulically compacted material subject to agreement.

Shall consist of No. 2 unalloyed copper wire scrap nodules, chopped or shredded, minimum 97% copper. Maximum metal impurities not to exceed 0.50% aluminum and 1% each of other metals or insulation. Hydraulically compacted material subject to agreement.

Shall consist of unalloyed copper wire scrap nodules, chopped or shredded, minimum 99% copper. Free of excessive insulation and other non-metallics. Maximum metal impurities: Aluminum 0.05%, Tin 0.25%, Nickel 0.05%, Antimony unspecified. Hydraulically compacted material subject to agreement.

Shall consist of miscellaneous, unalloyed copper scrap having a nominal 92% copper content (minimum 88%) as determined by electrolytic assay and shall consist of sheet copper, gutters, downspouts, kettles, boilers, and similar scrap. Must be free of burnt hair wire, copper clad, plating racks, grindings, burning copper wire with insulation, radiators, fire extinguishers, refrigerator units, electrotype shells, screening, excessively leaded, tinned, soldered scrap, brasses, bronzes, excessive oil, iron, and non-metallics; and should be reasonably free of ash. Hydraulically briquetted copper subject to agreement. Items excluded in this grade are also excluded in the higher grades.

Shall contain a minimum of 61.3% copper and maximum 5% iron; and consist of brass and bronze solids and turnings, as well as alloyed and contaminated copper scrap. Must be free of insulated wire, grindings, electrotype shells, and non-metallics. Hydraulically briquetted material subject to agreement.

Shall consist of No. 2 copper wire (see Birch) with various types of insulation. To be sold on a sample or recovery basis, subject to agreement between buyer and seller. Existence of jelly wire subject to agreement.

Shall consist of miscellaneous copper-containing skimmings, grindings, ashes, irony brass and copper residues and slags. Must be free of insulated wires; copper chlorides; unprepared tangled material; large motors; pyrophoric material; asbestos brake linings; furnace bottoms; high lead materials; graphite crucibles; and noxious/explosive materials. Fine powdered material by agreement. Hydraulically briquetted material subject to agreement.

Shall consist of No. 1 bare, uncoated, unalloyed copper wire (see Barley), not smaller than No. 16 B & S wire gauge (unless a smaller gauge is mutually agreed upon), with various types of insulation. To be sold on a sample or recovery basis, subject to agreement.

Shall consist of red brass scrap, valves, machinery bearings, and other machinery parts (including miscellaneous castings made of copper, tin, zinc, and/or lead). Must be free of semi-red brass castings (78–81% copper); railroad car boxes; and similar high-lead alloys; cocks, faucets, closed water meters, gates, pot pieces, ingots, burned brass; aluminum, silicon, and manganese bronzes; iron and non-metallics. No piece over 12" in any dimension or over 100 lbs. Heavier pieces acceptable upon mutual agreement.

Shall consist of scrap castings alloyed with copper, tin, bismuth, and zinc. Must be free of leaded brass attachments and have less than 0.2% alloyed lead (or as agreed). Examples include, but are not limited to, CDA 89833/35/36/37/41/42 and 45.

Shall consist of scrap borings and turnings alloyed with copper, tin, bismuth, and zinc. Must be unmixed and have less than 0.2% alloyed lead (or as agreed). Examples include, but are not limited to, CDA 89833/35/36/37/41/42 and 45.

It is recommended that these materials be sold by analysis.

Shall consist of red brass bushings and bearings from automobiles and other machinery, containing not less than 12% high tin-base babbitt and free of iron-backed bearings.

It is recommended these materials be sold on a sample or analysis basis.

To consist of 1000 and/or 3000 series alloys, free of paper, plastic, excessively inked sheets, and any other contaminants. Minimum size of 3” (8 cm) in any direction.

To consist of 1000 and/or 3000 series alloys, uncoated and unpainted, free of paper, plastic, ink, and any other contaminants. Minimum size of 3” (8 cm) in any direction.

Shall consist of new, clean, uncoated, and unpainted low copper aluminum scrap of two or more alloys with a minimum thickness of 0.015 inches (.38 mm) and free of 2000 and 7000 series, hair wire, wire screen, punchings less than 1/2 inch (1.25 cm) in diameter, dirt, and other non-metallic items. Grease and oil not to total more than 1%. Variations to be agreed upon.

Shall consist of clean old alloy aluminum sheet of two or more alloys, free of foil, venetian blinds, castings, hair wire, screen wire, food or beverage containers, radiator shells, airplane sheet, bottle caps, plastic, dirt, and other non-metallic items. Oil and grease not to total more than 1%. Up to 10% tale permitted.

Shall consist of new low copper aluminum can stock and clippings, clean (whether lithographed or not) and coated with clear lacquer but free of lids with sealers, iron, dirt, and other foreign contamination. Oil not to exceed 1%.

Shall consist of old aluminum food and/or beverage cans, free of other scrap metals, foil, tin cans, plastic bottles, paper, glass, and other non-metallic items. Variations to be agreed upon prior to shipment.

Shall have a density of 12–17 pounds per cubic foot (193–273 kg/m³). Material should contain a maximum of 5% fines (less than 4 mesh / 6.35 mm). Must be magnetically separated and free of steel, lead, bottle caps, plastic cans, other plastics, glass, wood, dirt, grease, trash, and other foreign substances. Any free lead is grounds for rejection.

Shall have a biscuit density of 35–50 pounds per cubic foot (562–802 kg/m³) minimum. Biscuit sizes range from 10”–13” x ~10¼” up to 20” x 6¼” x 9” with specified bundle dimensions and tying requirements. Must be magnetically separated and free of unwanted materials. Variations to be agreed upon.

Shall have a minimum density of 14 lb/ft³ (225 kg/m³) and a maximum density (varies by flattening). Bale dimensions and tying methods are specified. Must be magnetically separated and free of steel, lead, plastic, glass, wood, dirt, grease, and other contaminants. Variations to be agreed upon.

Shall have a briquette density of at least 50 lb/ft³ (800 kg/m³). Briquettes should be uniformly sized, bundled/stacked on skids, and secured by specified steel strapping. Must be magnetically separated and free of steel, plastic, glass, dirt, and other foreign substances. Variations to be agreed upon.

Shall consist of miscellaneous old zinc base die castings, with or without iron and other attachments. Must be free of borings, turnings, dross pieces, chunks, melted pieces, and skimmings. Unmeltables, dirt, and volatile substances are deductible. Material with over 30% iron will not constitute good delivery.

Shall consist of new or unused, clean zinc base die castings. Castings must be unplated, unpainted, and free from corrosion.

Shall consist of clean, old or used zinc base die cast automotive grilles, free from soldered material. All foreign attachments and extraneous materials are deductible.

Shall consist of new or unused, clean, plated zinc base die castings, free from corrosion.

Shall consist of clean, dry scrap zinc such as sheets, jar lids, clean unalloyed castings, and anti-corrosion plates. Borings and turnings are not acceptable. Material must not be excessively corroded or oxidized.

Shall consist of new pure zinc sheets or stampings free from corrosion, containing no foreign material or attachments. Printers zinc (e.g., engravers zinc, lithograph sheets, addressograph plates) are subject to special arrangements.

As produced from automobile fragmentizers, it must be clean and free of dirt, oil, glass, rubber, and trash. It should contain a maximum of 5% unmeltables such as free iron, copper, aluminum, etc.

Produced from automobile fragmentizers, material should contain about 55% zinc-bearing scrap with other nonferrous metals (e.g., aluminum, stainless steel, red metal) around 40% and insulated copper wire about 1%. Contaminants must not exceed 5%.

Shall consist only of galvanizers’ unsweated zinc dross in slab form from hot dip galvanizing, with a minimum zinc content of 92%. Must be free of skimmings and tramp iron. Broken pieces under 2” must not exceed 10% of shipment weight; slabs should not weigh over 100 lbs each.

Shall consist of melted zinc base die cast materials in smooth, clean solid slabs or pigs. Must be free from drosses, contain a minimum of 90% zinc, and have nickel and lead within specified limits.

Shall consist of unsweated zinc dross removed from the top of a continuous galvanizing bath, in slab form not exceeding 100 lbs each, with a minimum zinc content of 90%. Must be free of skimmings; broken pieces under 2” not to exceed 10%.

Shall consist of unsweated zinc dross removed from the bottom of a continuous galvanizing bath, in slab form not exceeding 100 lbs each, with a minimum zinc content of 92%. Must be free of skimmings; broken pieces under 2” not to exceed 10%.

Shall consist of metal skimmed from the top of a pot of molten zinc die cast metal. It must be unsweated, unfluxed, shiny, smooth, and free from corrosion or oxidation. It should be poured in molds or in small mounds not exceeding 75 lbs each.

Shall consist of clean magnesium clips in crucible size, free of copper, aluminum, and zinc flashings, and excessive oil/grease. Must be free of all foreign attachments.

Shall consist of magnesium castings, engine blocks, transmission casings, car wheels, extrusions, and sheet. Must be free from brass and copper inserts and foreign attachments. Larger pieces subject to negotiation.

To be free of copper, aluminum, zinc, and electrotype plates; must be clean and free of any foreign attachments. Shipped loose by agreement.

Shall consist of clean magnesium dockboards cut or broken to agreed size, and must be free of all foreign attachments.

It is recommended that these materials be sold by special arrangement between buyer and seller.

Shall consist of clean soft scrap lead, free of drosses, battery plates, lead-covered cable, hard lead, collapsible tubes, foil, type metals, aluminum, zinc, iron, brass fittings, dirty chemical lead, and radioactive materials.

Shall consist of clean lead solids and lead shots, free of other materials such as drosses, battery plates, lead-covered cable, collapsible tubes, type metals, aluminum, zinc, iron, and brass fittings, and chemical contaminants.

Must be free of any liquid. Cases must be complete (plastic or rubber) including caps. Non-lead alternatives (nicad, ni-fe, etc.) are not acceptable.

To be free of scrap lead, wheel weights, battery plates, rubber/plastic case material, and other foreign material. Must have a minimum of 97% metallic content.

To consist of nonferrous wheel weights with at least 35% nonferrous content and no more than 65% iron. Final specifications are to be determined between buyer and seller.

Must be free of armored covered cable and any foreign material; specifications to be negotiated.

Should be clean and reasonably free of other materials such as iron, dirt, or chemicals. Must be free of radioactive materials, aluminum, and zinc. May be sold on an assay basis.

Consisting of automotive, truck, 8-D, and commercial batteries with plastic or rubber cases. Non-lead types (ni-cad, etc.) are not acceptable.

Consisting of plates enclosed in a complete plastic case. Partial, cracked, or broken cells and variations subject to special agreement.

Consisting of bus, diesel, locomotive, telephone, and/or steel-cased batteries. Submarine batteries and partial/broken units subject to negotiation.

To consist of lead wheel weights (with or without iron clips) and must be free of foreign material. Final specifications to be determined between buyer and seller.

Shall consist of clean new sheet, plate, bar, tube, and other wrought nickel scrap solids. Must have a minimum of 99% nickel; cobalt maximum 0.25%; copper maximum 0.50%. Free of castings and foreign attachments.

Shall consist of old and/or new sheet, plate, bar, tube, and other wrought nickel scrap solids. Must contain at least 98% nickel and a maximum of 0.50% copper. Free of castings, soldered, brazed, sweated, or painted material.

Shall consist of clean, new, segregated cupro nickel tube, pipe, sheet, plate, or other solid forms (70/30, 80/20, or 90/10). Must be free of foreign attachments.

Shall consist of old and/or new segregated cupro nickel tube, pipe, sheet, plate, or other solid forms (70/30, 80/20, or 90/10) with a maximum of 2% sediment allowable. Other forms (castings, gates, etc.) may be included only upon agreement.

Shall consist of clean, segregated cupro nickel spinnings, turnings, or borings (70/30, 80/20, or 90/10). Specifications to be agreed upon.

Shall consist of segregated cupro nickel solids (70/30, 80/20, or 90/10), soldered, brazed, or sweated. Must be free of trimmed seams, edges, and other contamination.

Shall consist of miscellaneous scrap where nickel and copper are the basic elements (e.g., peelings, plating racks, hangers). To be sold by description and analysis.

Shall consist of clean, new R-Monel sheet, plate, bar, rod, tube, or other wrought scrap, free of foreign attachments.

Shall consist of new, clean R and K-Monel solids and clippings. Must be free of cast material and foreign attachments.

Shall consist of clean R and K-Monel solids (sheet, plate, pipe, rods, forgings, screen, and wire cloth). Must be free of soldered, brazed, welded, or sweated material and other contamination.

Shall consist of clean K-Monel solids.

Shall consist of soldered and/or brazed miscellaneous Monel alloys in wrought or cast form, free of trimmed seams, non-metallic filling, and foreign attachments.

Shall consist of various clean Monel castings with a minimum of 60% nickel. Must be free of foreign attachments.

Shall consist of mixed Monel turnings and borings containing at least 60% nickel content, on a dry basis.

Shall consist of clean AISI Series Stainless Steel Scrap Solids containing a maximum of 0.5% copper, free of foreign attachments and other contamination.

Shall consist of clean 18-8 type stainless steel clips and solids containing a minimum of 7% nickel, 16% chrome, with limits on molybdenum, copper, phosphorous, and sulfur. Specifications to be agreed upon.

Any nickel-bearing alloy scrap that contains at least 3% recovered nickel. Nickel content determined by recovery after buyer’s evaluation; balance of elements to be discussed.

Shall consist of clean 18-8 stainless steel turnings containing a minimum of 7% nickel and 16% chrome, free of nonferrous metals, non-metallics, excessive iron, oil, and other contaminants.

Nickel-iron batteries to be sold free of crates, copper terminal connectors, and excess liquid; must be free of nickel-cadmium batteries.

Shall consist of a combination of stainless steel, insulated copper wire, aluminum, copper, lead, magnesium, nickel, tin, and zinc in elemental or alloyed form. The percentages are to be agreed upon. Material is produced by sensor sorting (e.g., induction or X-ray) and must be free of radioactive material, dross, or ash.

Shall consist of whole, complete fluorescent light ballasts containing copper inside. Must not contain polychlorinated biphenyls (PCBs). Electronic ballasts subject to agreement.

Shall consist of whole steel-cased compressors (from air conditioner units, freezers, refrigerators, etc.) containing a motor. Must be free of hazardous materials (e.g., CFCs, PCBs) and loose or extra iron attachments.

Shall consist of whole electric motors and/or dismantled parts that are primarily copper-wound. May include some aluminum-wound material. No excessive steel attachments; sealed units or cast iron compressors are excluded.

Sized to approximately basketball size or smaller, these shall consist of whole electric motors and/or dismantled parts that are primarily copper-wound. Specifications similar to Elmo MIXED ELECTRIC MOTORS apply.

Shall consist of mixed copper and aluminum bearing material from ferrous shredding, comprised of motors without cases. May include insulated copper harness wire. Trace contaminants and fines may be present.

Similar to Sheema, but may contain up to 5% aluminum-wound material and insulated copper harness wire. Trace contaminants may be present. No free iron or sealed units.

Block tin must assay a minimum of 98% tin and be free of liquids, solder, brass connections, pewter, pumps, pot pieces, and dirt.

Shall consist of tableware and soda-fountain boxes with a minimum of 84% tin. Siphon tops to be accounted for separately. Must be free of brass, zinc, and other foreign metals.

Shall contain a minimum of 78% tin and be free of brassy or zincy metals.

Shall consist of lead-base bearing metal containing not less than 8% tin, free of Allens metal, ornamental, antimonial, and type metal. Must be free of all zinc and excessive copper in the alloy.
