Scope. BR-Index is a family of daily scrap benchmarks. Series definitions (symbol/region/material/currency) are listed at /indices/universe. All calculations use UTC; the as-of date equals trade date.

Inputs & Eligibility

  • Vendor submissions/quotes meeting onboarding checks.
  • Internal reference component derived from related metals (stabilizer only).
  • Operational signals (BOL/contract events) where applicable.

Duplicates, stale (>5 business days), obvious errors, or non-arm’s-length prints are excluded.

Calculation (Blended)

index_price = w_ref * reference_component + w_vendor * vendor_blend
w_ref + w_vendor = 1.0; defaults: w_ref=0.70, w_vendor=0.30

Vendor blend: volume-weighted mean after cleaning/outlier filters. Rounding: nearest $0.50 unless series specifies otherwise.

Outliers & Minimums

  • Z-score ±2.5 and IQR fences; down-weight or exclude
  • Publish if ≥3 eligible vendors, else fallback reduces vendor weight

Fallbacks & Holidays

Insufficient contributors → clamp vendor weight (e.g., 0.10) and increase reference component. US federal banking holidays: prior business day carries.

Corrections & Restatements

Material input errors may trigger restatement (generally within T+10 business days). Incidents are logged at /indices/incidents.

Governance & Transparency

  • Oversight: Index Governance Committee (/governance)
  • Change control & notices: /indices/changes.json
  • Run-level lineage: each row embeds run_id and method_rev; see /indices/runs/{run_id}
