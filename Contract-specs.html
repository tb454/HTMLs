Copper Futures - Contract Specifications

Contract Details

  • Contract Unit: 25,000 pounds (Grade 1 Electrolytic Copper)
  • Price Quotation: U.S. dollars and cents per pound (USD/lb)
  • Minimum Price Fluctuation: $0.0005 per pound = $12.50 per contract
    (TAS/TAM: Zero or ±10 ticks in the minimum tick increment of the outright; Spot TAS: Zero)
  • Trading Hours:
    • CME Globex (ET): Sunday - Friday 6:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m. with a 60-minute break each day beginning at 5:00 p.m.
    • TAS: Sunday - Friday 6:00 p.m. - 1:00 p.m. (ET)
    • TAM: Sunday - Friday 6:00 p.m. ET - 12:35 p.m. London time
    • CME ClearPort (CT): Sunday 5:00 p.m. - Friday 4:00 p.m. with no reporting Mon - Thu from 4:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m.
    Note: Times are subject to change; always check the CME Group website or official exchange notices for the most current trading schedule.
  • Product Code:
    • CME Globex: HGC
    • CME ClearPort: HGClearing
    • TAS: "HGT","HG0"
    • TAM: HGF

Listed Contracts

  • Monthly contracts listed for 24 consecutive months and any Mar, May, Jul, Sep, and Dec in the nearest 63 months.

Settlement & Termination

  • Settlement Method: Deliverable (physical delivery of copper)
  • Termination of Trading:
    • Trading terminates at 12:00 Noon CT on the third-last business day of the contract month.
    • TAM trading terminates at 12:35 p.m. London time on the third-last business day of the month prior to the contract month.
  • TAS/TAM Rules:
    Trading at Settlement (TAS) is subject to Rule 524.A. TAS trades off a “Base Price” of zero (equal to the daily settlement price) to create a differential versus the daily settlement price of the underlying futures contract month. The TAS clearing price equals the daily settlement price plus or minus the TAS transaction price.
    Trading at Marker (TAM) works similarly, using a marker price rather than the settlement price to establish the differential.

  • Delivery Period:
    Delivery can occur any business day beginning on the first business day of the delivery month, up to and including the last business day of the current delivery month.
  • Grade and Quality:
    Copper must be Grade 1 electrolytic copper cathodes (full plate or cut) conforming to ASTM B115-00 or its latest revision.
